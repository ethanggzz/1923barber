Who we are

Beaumont's home for the craft

1923 Barber Parlor is more than a barbershop — it's a community. Founded on the belief that everyone deserves to look and feel their best, we've built a space in Beaumont where skill meets comfort and every client leaves satisfied.

Veteran barbers with 15+ years experience — Krystal and the team have been perfecting their craft for over a decade and a half.

Inclusive and welcoming environment — patient with children, experienced with all hair textures, and accommodating for clients with special needs.

Booth rentals available — experienced barbers looking for a professional home can reach out about joining the team.
